About Me

My name is Michael Dubois and I am currently enrolled in a UNC bootcamp to become a full-stack developer. I was raised in North Carolina, and upon graduating high school, joined the United States Air Force as a Network Intelligence Analyst. I was Active Duty for six years, the entirety of which was at Lackland, AFB in San Antonio, Texas. Afterwards, I worked as a U.S. Army Contractor for 2 years in the same career field.

My career with the military solidified my interest in computing, development, and engineering and helped jumpstart my skillbase. I was afforded a lot of opportunities, which granted me a basic knowledge of R, Python, and CCENT. When not nerding out, I enjoy cooking and spending time with my two dogs, Ruger and Karma.
